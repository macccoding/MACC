# Image Generation Prompts — "The Path Isn't Straight"

> **Story:** Mike's non-linear journey — from Mandeville, Jamaica to building across countries. Photography, logistics, code, 3D printing, travel — it looks scattered but it compounds into something real.
> **Style:** Sumi-e ink wash base with Ookami-level drama — bold calligraphic strokes, celestial motifs, selective vermillion/gold bursts, flowing nature elements, atmospheric negative space.
> **Palette:** Warm parchment (#F5EDE0), sumi ink (#1A1814), vermillion red (#D03A2C), gold seal (#C9A84C)
> **NOT:** Western woodcut, clean digital art, thick blocky linocut, Ralph Steadman

---

## Global Style Directive (include in every prompt)

```
Japanese sumi-e ink wash painting style with Okami video game influence.
Bold calligraphic brush strokes on warm aged parchment/washi paper texture.
Atmospheric negative space. Ink bleeds and washes creating depth through
transparency. Selective vermillion red (#D03A2C) and gold (#C9A84C) accents
against monochrome ink. Celestial/divine motifs — swirling clouds, flowing
water, wind trails. NOT clean digital art. NOT Western woodcut. The brush
work should feel confident, fast, and alive — like a master calligrapher
working with sumi ink on rice paper. Visible paper grain texture.
```

---

## THE STORY ARC

```
ACT 1 — "The Origin" (parchment bg, sumi ink)
  Where I started. Small island. Big appetite.

ACT 2 — "The Winding" (ink wash deepens, vermillion appears)
  The path twists. Many directions at once. Doubt.

ACT 3 — "The Dark Water" (deep ink wash, near black)
  The seasons where nothing made sense.

ACT 4 — "The Brush Finds Its Stroke" (vermillion + gold emerge)
  When you stop trying to go straight and trust the winding.

ACT 5 — "Experience. Build. Move." (full palette, celestial)
  The compound effect. Everything connects.
```
